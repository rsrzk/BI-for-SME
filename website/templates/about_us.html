About Us


Our Story


We are a team of data enthusiasts based in Kuala Lumpur looking to help SMEs with their data needs. It started with us working closely with the owners of a boutique, Sarah and Mas (By Sarah Ahmad), to study the feasibility of using their sales data to apply for loans. We made some fancy graphs and threw in a bit of forecasting (so we could say we used AI) to come up with a dashboard for banks to use. But one of the owners, Mas, then said to us “I think your tool can actually help me run my business” which has now led us down a different path to where we are today – working directly with business owners to build tools and reports that can allow them to make better decisions.
